---
title: "Why Physics First"
date: "2026-07-03"
description: "On starting with physics before technology and everything downstream of it."
tags: ["Physics", "Technology"]
---

Most conversations about technology start in the middle — with the app,
the model, the interface — and skip past the physical constraints that
actually shape what's possible.

I like starting further back. Before there's a product, there's a
material, a signal, a rate of heat dissipation. Understanding the
physics underneath doesn't just satisfy curiosity; it changes what
questions you think to ask about the technology built on top of it.

That's the lens this blog will keep coming back to: physics as the
foundation, technology as the application, and society as the place
where the consequences actually land.
